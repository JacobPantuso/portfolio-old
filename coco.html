Coco

Let's make productivity simpler

Productivity apps
should be simple

That’s why we created an app where users can manage upcoming events and tasks with zero complications. Tired of staring at a calendar with dots? Well, we have you covered! With our at a glance home view, users can view upcoming reminders and events in a clean and simple layout.

Zero Ads
Zero Fees

Coco is completely free of additional fees and ads. Nobody likes an app that constantly bugs you with ad popups or “premium features”. This is another reason why Coco should be your go-to app for productivity management.

Secure data storage
and backups

Coco uses your iCloud storage to securely store your calendar data. It uses the Secure Enclave features from Apple to protect your privacy if you don’t have any iCloud storage available. This way your information always stays protected.

Works with the
services you love

Want to make a shortcut to enable an iPhone focus mode when an event is beginning? Link your calendar with IFTTT? Use Siri to add events and reminders? Link email acounts for automatic event recognoition? Well, you can do it all with Coco.
